BreakJS - Breakout-style JavaScript Game

Begin the game by click in the canvas. The ball we begin moving. Reflect the ball off your paddle in the hopes of hitting the bricks to make them disappear. Each brick that is hit will reward you with points. The game ends when all of the bricks are gone
